About Us

Technical Club is one of the most active societies aiming to promote and encourage technical innovation, practical experience in the modern technologies among students by organizing Workshops, Technical Seminars, Training Courses, etc. Technical Club also organizes the National Level Annual Techno-Cultural Festival ProdyoGeeky at NIT Uttarakhand. Technical Club will organize various technical competitions throughout the year related to Innovation, Robotics, Coding, Programming, Modelling, Designing, Gaming, and Quiz. It also provides a platform to the students who are very much interested in coding and innovations, ethical hacking, web development, mobile application development, etc. Technical Club also serves as the parent club to three coding clubs, SAE India Collegiate Club and Robotics Club to oversee the various activities taking place through these clubs.
